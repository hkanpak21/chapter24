## About

Gordon McKay Professor of Computer Science at Harvard. Works across cryptography, complexity theory, and algorithmic statistics. Known for sum-of-squares algorithms, cryptographic obfuscation, and complexity-theoretic reductions.

## Contributions / Works

- **[A Nearly Tight Sum-of-Squares Lower Bound for the Planted Clique Problem](../sources/barak-sos-lower-bound-planted-clique.md)** (Barak, Hopkins, Kelner, Kothari, Moitra, Potechin; FOCS 2016 / SICOMP 2019) — pseudo-calibration for SOS lower bounds.
- Applebaum-Barak-Wigderson 2010 — PKE from a non-standard planted-clique variant; predecessor to STOC 2025 result that achieved PKE from standard PC.
