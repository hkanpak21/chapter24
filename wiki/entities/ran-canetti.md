## About
Professor at Boston University and Tel Aviv University; foundational cryptographer known for the Universal Composability (UC) framework, secure multi-party computation, and formal definitions of cryptographic security. Gödel Prize winner. Extended his formal-definitions program into learning theory with covert learning.

## Contributions / Works
- [covert-learning](../sources/covert-learning.md) (with Ari Karchmer, 2021) — formal definition and constructions of covert PAC learning.

## Connections
- Co-author: [Ari Karchmer](ari-karchmer.md).
- Methodologically aligned with game-based security tradition used by [Shafi Goldwasser](shafi-goldwasser.md) and [Vinod Vaikuntanathan](vinod-vaikuntanathan.md).
