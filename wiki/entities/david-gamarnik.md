## About

Professor at MIT Sloan (Operations Research). Developer of the Overlap Gap Property (OGP) as a tool for proving algorithmic hardness of random optimization problems.

## Contributions / Works

- Overlap Gap Property line of work (with Zadik and others) — rules out local / stable algorithms for a broad class of random optimization problems.
- **[Hardness of Random Optimization Problems for Boolean Circuits, Low-Degree Polynomials, and Langevin Dynamics](../sources/gamarnik-hardness-random-optimization-circuits.md)** (Gamarnik, Jagannath, Wein; SICOMP 2024).
- **[Circuit Lower Bounds for the p-Spin Optimization Problem](../sources/gamarnik-circuit-lower-bounds-pspin.md)** (Gamarnik, Jagannath, Wein).
