## About

Principal researcher working on the theory of machine learning, optimization, and adversarial robustness. Known for foundational contributions to convex optimization and, more recently, formal complexity-theoretic results on the necessity of overparameterization for robustness.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Universal Law of Robustness via Isoperimetry](../sources/universal-law-of-robustness.md)** (NeurIPS 2021, Outstanding Paper) — with Mark Sellke
- "A Law of Robustness beyond Isoperimetry" (arXiv:2202.11592, 2022) — extends to non-isoperimetric settings
- Prior work: "Convex Optimization: Algorithms and Complexity" (2015 monograph); online convex optimization
