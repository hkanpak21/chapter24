## About

Founder of the Alignment Research Center (ARC) and former head of language model alignment at OpenAI. Designed the foundational RLHF approach to LLM alignment. Since ~2023 has focused on theoretical foundations of AI alignment including backdoor defendability and mechanistic interpretability.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Backdoor Defense, Learnability, and Obfuscation (CHLX25)](../sources/backdoor-defense-learnability-obfuscation.md)** (ITCS 2025, first author) — introduces the defendability framework; proves learnable ⊊ defendable ⊊ all; iO non-defendability of polynomial circuits.
- Foundational RLHF work (Christiano et al. 2017): Deep Reinforcement Learning from Human Preferences (NeurIPS 2017).
- Eliciting Latent Knowledge research agenda at ARC.
- Recent focus on mechanistic interpretability and alignment foundations.

## Connections

- Jacob Hilton, Victor Lecomte, Mark Xu — ARC co-authors on CHLX25.
- Authored foundational RLHF work with Ziegler, Irving, Brown, Sutskever, Amodei.
