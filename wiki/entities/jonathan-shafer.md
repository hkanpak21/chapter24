## About

Researcher at MIT; works on the complexity-theoretic foundations of machine learning, including backdoor defenses, computational learning theory, and the intersection of cryptography with statistical learning.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Oblivious Defense in ML Models (GSVV25)](../sources/oblivious-defense-backdoor-removal.md)** (STOC 2025) — establishes that backdoors can be provably mitigated even when cryptographically undetectable; introduces defensibility-vs-detectability split; global mitigation for Fourier-heavy functions and local mitigation for linear/polynomial functions.
