## About

Theoretical computer science researcher (University of Virginia, then University of Oklahoma) working on learning theory and adversarial robustness. Frequent collaborator of Saeed Mahloujifar and Mohammad Mahmoody on concentration-of-measure based adversarial ML.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Curse of Concentration (MDM19)](../sources/curse-of-concentration.md)** — general concentration-to-adversarial-examples theorem
- [DMM18]: Adversarial risk and robustness definitions, hypercube adversarial examples (NeurIPS 2018)
- Work on PAC-style learning under adversarial perturbations
