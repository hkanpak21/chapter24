## About

Associate Professor at NYU Courant Institute. Works on mathematical foundations of deep learning, high-dimensional probability, and the statistical-computational interface.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Continuous LWE](../sources/bruna-continuous-lwe.md)** (Bruna, Regev, Song, Tang; STOC 2021) — bridges lattice hardness to statistical learning.
- **[Lattice-Based Methods Surpass Sum-of-Squares in Clustering](../sources/zadik-lattice-surpass-sos.md)** (Zadik, Song, Wein, Bruna; COLT 2022) — algorithmic counterexample to low-degree/SOS hardness predictions.
