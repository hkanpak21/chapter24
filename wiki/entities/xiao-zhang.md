## About

Researcher at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security; PhD from University of Virginia (advised by David Evans). Works on empirical and theoretical aspects of ML robustness, with an emphasis on concentration-of-measure based intrinsic-robustness analyses.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Empirically Measuring Concentration (MZME19)](../sources/empirically-measuring-concentration.md)** (NeurIPS 2019, equal first author with Saeed Mahloujifar) — first provably-convergent algorithm to estimate the concentration function of a data distribution from samples; empirical evidence that the concentration-based intrinsic-robustness bound is not tight on MNIST / CIFAR-10.
- Follow-up work on manifold-aware concentration estimation and intrinsic robustness measurement via generative models.
