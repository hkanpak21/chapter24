## About

Professor at NYU Courant Institute. Gödel Prize laureate for introducing the Learning With Errors (LWE) problem (STOC 2005). Foundational figure in lattice-based cryptography and the worst-case-to-average-case reduction that underpins most post-quantum cryptography.

## Contributions / Works

- Regev 2005 — introduces LWE; first worst-case-to-average-case reduction from lattice problems.
- **[Continuous LWE](../sources/bruna-continuous-lwe.md)** (Bruna, Regev, Song, Tang; STOC 2021) — connects lattice hardness to statistical learning.
