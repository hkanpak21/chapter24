## About

PhD student at MIT. Works on the cryptographic foundations of ML security, backdoor attacks and defenses, and the interplay between cryptographic hardness and statistical learning.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Oblivious Defense in ML Models (GSVV25)](../sources/oblivious-defense-backdoor-removal.md)** (STOC 2025) — backdoor mitigation without detection.
- **Statistically Undetectable Backdoors in Deep Neural Networks** (forthcoming, with Bogdanov, Rosen) — strengthens GKVZ22 to *statistical* undetectability in the white-box setting; uses cryptographic view of Johnson-Lindenstrauss.
