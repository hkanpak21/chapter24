## About

Turing Award winner (2012, with Silvio Micali). Professor at UC Berkeley (and previously MIT). Pioneer of modern cryptography — co-inventor of probabilistic encryption, zero-knowledge proofs, and interactive proof systems. Recent work applies cryptographic methodology to ML security.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Planting Undetectable Backdoors in ML Models](../sources/planting-undetectable-backdoors.md)** (FOCS 2022) — with Kim, Vaikuntanathan, Zamir
- Co-inventor of zero-knowledge proofs, probabilistic encryption, GMW protocol, IP = PSPACE, PCP theorem (co-developer)
