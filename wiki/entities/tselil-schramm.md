## About

Assistant Professor of Statistics at Stanford. Works on statistical-computational tradeoffs, sum-of-squares lower bounds, and high-dimensional statistics.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Franz-Parisi Criterion and Computational Trade-offs in High Dimensional Statistics](../sources/bandeira-franz-parisi-criterion.md)** (Bandeira, El Alaoui, Hopkins, Schramm, Wein, Zadik) — physics-inspired criterion for computational hardness.
- **[SQ Algorithms and Low-Degree Tests Are Almost Equivalent](../sources/brennan-sq-lowdegree-equivalence.md)** (Brennan, Bresler, Hopkins, Li, Schramm) — formal equivalence between SQ and low-degree frameworks.
