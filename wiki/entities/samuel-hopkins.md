## About

Assistant Professor at MIT EECS. Works on algorithmic statistical-computational tradeoffs, robust estimation, and sum-of-squares. Originator of the low-degree conjecture framework as a predictive tool for computational-statistical hardness (Hopkins thesis 2018).

## Contributions / Works

- **[A Nearly Tight Sum-of-Squares Lower Bound for the Planted Clique Problem](../sources/barak-sos-lower-bound-planted-clique.md)** (Barak, Hopkins, Kelner, Kothari, Moitra, Potechin; FOCS 2016 / SICOMP 2019) — pseudo-calibration technique.
- **[SQ Algorithms and Low-Degree Tests Are Almost Equivalent](../sources/brennan-sq-lowdegree-equivalence.md)** (Brennan, Bresler, Hopkins, Li, Schramm; COLT 2021 Best Paper Runner-Up).
- **[Franz-Parisi Criterion](../sources/bandeira-franz-parisi-criterion.md)** (Bandeira, El Alaoui, Hopkins, Schramm, Wein, Zadik).
- 2018 thesis originating the low-degree conjecture.
- Robust estimation: SSE-hardness connections for robust estimation.
