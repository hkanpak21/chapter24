## About

Assistant Professor of Statistics and Data Science at Yale. Works on statistical-computational tradeoffs with emphasis on algorithmic counterexamples to low-degree/SQ hardness predictions.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Lattice-Based Methods Surpass Sum-of-Squares in Clustering](../sources/zadik-lattice-surpass-sos.md)** (Zadik, Song, Wein, Bruna; COLT 2022) — lattice algorithms solve clustering problems where SOS provably fails, demonstrating low-degree frameworks miss algebraic structure.
- **[Franz-Parisi Criterion](../sources/bandeira-franz-parisi-criterion.md)** (Bandeira, El Alaoui, Hopkins, Schramm, Wein, Zadik) — physics-to-complexity bridge for hardness.
- Overlap Gap Property line of work (with Gamarnik).
