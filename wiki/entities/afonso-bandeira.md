## About

Professor of Mathematics at ETH Zürich. Works on mathematical data science, random matrix theory, and statistical-computational tradeoffs.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Franz-Parisi Criterion and Computational Trade-offs in High Dimensional Statistics](../sources/bandeira-franz-parisi-criterion.md)** (Bandeira, El Alaoui, Hopkins, Schramm, Wein, Zadik) — lead author on the physics-inspired computational-hardness criterion.
