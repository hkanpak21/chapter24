## About

Researcher in the theory of adversarially robust machine learning. Key contributor to characterizing the sample complexity of robust PAC learning.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Adversarially Robust Learning: Characterization](../sources/robust-pac-learning-characterization.md)** (NeurIPS 2022) — global one-inclusion graph dimension with Hanneke and Srebro
- **[VC Classes are Adversarially Robustly Learnable, but Only Improperly](../sources/vc-classes-adversarially-robustly-learnable.md)** (COLT 2019) — with Hanneke and Srebro; foundational proper/improper separation
