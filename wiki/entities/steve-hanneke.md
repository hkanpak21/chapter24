## About

Researcher at TTIC (Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago). Leading researcher in PAC learning theory, active learning, and adversarially robust learning.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Adversarially Robust Learning: Characterization](../sources/robust-pac-learning-characterization.md)** (NeurIPS 2022) — with Montasser and Srebro
- **[VC Classes are Adversarially Robustly Learnable, but Only Improperly](../sources/vc-classes-adversarially-robustly-learnable.md)** (COLT 2019) — with Montasser and Srebro
- **[Robustly-Reliable Learners Under Poisoning Attacks](../sources/robustly-reliable-learners-poison.md)** (COLT 2022) — with Balcan, Blum, Sharma; correctness certificates against instance-targeted poisoning
- Extensive work on sample complexity of PAC learning, active learning, and domain adaptation
