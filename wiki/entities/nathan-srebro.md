## About

Professor at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC) and the University of Chicago. Leading researcher in statistical learning theory, generalization bounds, and adversarially robust learning.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Adversarially Robust Learning: Characterization](../sources/robust-pac-learning-characterization.md)** (NeurIPS 2022) — with Montasser and Hanneke
- **[VC Classes are Adversarially Robustly Learnable, but Only Improperly](../sources/vc-classes-adversarially-robustly-learnable.md)** (COLT 2019) — with Montasser and Hanneke
- Extensive work on generalization theory, matrix completion, implicit regularization in deep learning
