## About

Researcher in statistical computational complexity, particularly on low-degree polynomial methods for understanding statistical-computational tradeoffs.

## Contributions / Works

- **[Low-Degree Polynomials Survey](../sources/low-degree-polynomials-survey.md)** (arXiv:2506.10748, 2025) — comprehensive survey of the low-degree framework
- Key work on detection-recovery gaps for planted dense subgraph (Schramm and Wein, Ann. Statist. 2022)
- Brennan, Bresler, Hopkins, Li, Schramm, Wein (COLT 2021) — formal SQ = low-degree equivalence theorem
