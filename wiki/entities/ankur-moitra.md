## About

Professor of Mathematics at MIT. Works on algorithmic aspects of machine learning and robust statistics. Co-developer of the "algorithmic robust statistics" program alongside Diakonikolas, Kamath, Kane, Li, Stewart.

## Contributions / Works

- **[A Nearly Tight Sum-of-Squares Lower Bound for the Planted Clique Problem](../sources/barak-sos-lower-bound-planted-clique.md)** (Barak, Hopkins, Kelner, Kothari, Moitra, Potechin).
- **[Robust Estimators in High Dimensions without the Computational Intractability](../sources/)** (Diakonikolas, Kamath, Kane, Li, Moitra, Stewart; FOCS 2016) — foundational algorithmic robust statistics.
